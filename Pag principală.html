Beetlejuice the Musical

Beetlejuice is an English-language musical based on the 1988 film of the same name. It's about an unusual teenager, Lydia Deetz, whose life changes completely when she meets two recently deceased people and a demon.







Beetlejuice the Musical stars:

  1. Alex Brightman plays Beetlejuice.
  2. Elizabeth Teeter plays Lydia.
  3. Kerry Butler plays Barbara.
  4. David Josefsberg plays Adam.
  5. Leslie Kritzer plays Delia
  6. Adam Dannheisser plays Charles.
  7. Kelvin Loh plays Otho.
  8. Danny Rutigliano plays Maxie Dean
  9. Dana Steingold plays Dana Steingold.
  10. Zonya Love plays Maxine Dean, Juno

Beetlejuice in 1988

This Beetlejuice show was based on another movie of the same name released on March 30, 1988 (United States). The film can also be found under the name "Viaţă de stafie". This film directed by Tim Burton won the Academy Award for Best Makeup and the National Society of Film Critics Award for Best Actor. This film was appreciated by 82% of those who watched it.

The film lasts one hour and 32 minutes.

Description- Because of a puppy, the newlyweds Adam and Barbara Maitland are killed in a car accident and arrive in Heaven, where they have the unpleasant surprise of finding that the bureaucracy is in full swing, so they were put on a long waiting list . Until they receive their angel wings, for 50 years they must continue to live in the old house, but as ghosts. But the house is now occupied by a couple of yuppies, Charles and Delia Deetz, whom the ghosts are unable to scare one by one. So, in desperation for the cause, a specialist is hired for this - Beetlejuice, a red-haired guy with broken teeth, who turns out not to be up to the task, especially since he falls in love with the daughter of the new owners, the teenage Lydia.
