The Sims 4

The Sims 4 is a life simulation game. Players can create a Sim character and can also explore different personalities that change the way the game is played. Sims can multitask, such as talking while performing a task.







Creating a Sim

Players can directly manipulate a Sim's facial features. Players can also directly manipulate any part of the body, including the abdomen, chest, legs, and arms. This is a new feature and was not included in previous Sims games where only with fitness could fat be manipulated on a Sims body. However, fitness levels and fat levels can still be adjusted in The Sims 4 as in previous games. The base games come with over 40 hairstyles for men and women. Six life stages are available, including baby, child, teenager, young adult, adult and old man. The child's life stage is only accessible by birthing a Sim and is not available by creating a Sim. The features are back! Each Sim has three traits and an aspiration that contains their own hidden trait. Compared to previous Sims games where Casual, Formal, Sleep, Athletic, and Swimwear were restricted to having their own clothing options, all clothing options are now available in all forms of outfits and players are allowed up to 5 outfits per category.

Cheats

Are you running out of simoleons and can't pay your bills? Or did you age up and turn into an elder and you would like to be young again? The Sims 4 has a variety of in-build cheats that players can use to facilitate their game play. In order to use any cheat codes you need to activate the cheat console. You can do this by: If you are playing on a PS4 or XBOX, press all four shoulder buttons at once to open the cheat console. Press Ctrl+Shift+C while in game on a computer, or if you're on a Mac press Command+Shift+C. This will open a console window in the upper left corner. Enter the cheat code of your choice from the list below and then hit Enter. If you need to close the cheat console simply press Esc. There are different types of cheats:
  • Increase Family Funds
    kaching
    Your Family Funds will be increased by $1,000
  • Increase Family Funds
    rosebud
    Your Family Funds will be increased by $1,000
  • Increase Family Funds
    motherlode
    Your Family Funds will be increased by $50,000
  • Objects Can Be Placed Anywhere
    bb.moveobjects on
    You can now place objects anywhere on the lot
  • Reset stuck sim
    resetSim [firstname] [lastname]
    Resets the sim with given first name and last name by cancelling all his actions that may be preventing him from performing correctly.
  • Turn off death
    death.toggle true
    All sims will not die, regardless of what happens to them. They can not die of old age or fatal accidents.
  • Turn on death
    death.toggle false
    All sims will be able to die. This is the default value.
