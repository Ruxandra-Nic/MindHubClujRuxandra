Where is the theater and what hotels are there?

What city is it in?

Beetlejuice is a theater located in New York, so if you decide to go to it and need more detailed information, the theater is located in the Marquis Theater, 210 W 46th St, New York , NY 10036, United States of America. I still recommend this theater and if you want to know more about it and how to get there then you should keep reading.


Recommended hotels in the area



1.New York Marriott Marquis

The New York Marriott Marquis hotel is close to the Marquis Theater as the name suggests. About 12,222 reviews say it's a 4.4 star hotel.
Address - 1535 Broadway, New York, NY 10036, United States of America.
Phone Number - +12123981900.
Website - marriott.com .



2.Chelsea Savoy Hotel

A little further from the theater we have the Chelsea Savoy Hotel. According to tourists, the rooms are spacious and clean, although some said that they are old and that the maintenance could be improved. The bathrooms are spacious and clean. (3.8 stars). The hotel is close to public transport stations, the train station and the city center (4.8 stars). Guests appreciated the friendly staff (4.6 stars).
Address - 204 W 23rd St, New York, NY 10011, United States of America.
Phone Number - +1 2129299353.
Website - chelseasavoynyc.com.



3. Row NYC

Last but not least, we have Row NYC hotel. From 10,242 reviews, the hotel has 3.6 stars.
Address - 700 8th Ave, New York, NY 10036, United States of America.
Phone Number - +12128693600.
